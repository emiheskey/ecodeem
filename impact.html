IMPACT

Ecodeem is very keen on poverty alleviation and reducing extreme hunger by raising the living standards of over 2 million Africans above $2 per day as it is structured to create of over 2 million Jobs for African youths and at empowering over a thousand youths in recycling waste to reusable made in Africa products. The need to practically engage Africans in directly play an effective role at mitigating climate change and ensuring environmental protection across their urban and rural settlements is a major focus of the ecodeem project. Our project offers the African continent a range of socioeconomic benefits; from massive job creation to ecological benefits and internally generated revenues for governments across the continent as we also move towards easing the continents demand for energy by providing affordable access to solar power, inverters, windmills, solar lamps, electric vehicles etc. Users will receive reward package (based on the weight of the recyclable waste provided) into their Green accounts after 24hrs from pickup. We also seek to engage the use of green rangers who will be trained and equipped in collecting recyclables at our various locations around Africa even as it provides users with timely and affordable access to cleaner energy.

UNSKILLED VOLUNTEER EMPLOYMENT (Green Rangers):

2 million Africans will be employed as Green Rangers and are expected to be 18 years of age and above having a maximum academic requirement of college graduation. They will be responsible for the job of collecting recyclable waste from their designated stations. The project aims at rebranding scavenging by equipping our set of Green Rangers with personal protection equipment such as overall garments, hand gloves, raincoats, protective boots and health insurance. We will also employ over 20,000 van drivers spread across urban and rural settlements across Africa.

GRADUATE EMPLOYMENT (ECO AGENTS):

Over 100,000 university graduates will be employed across the continent to work as supervisors to coordinate the activities of the green rangers. These noble supervisors will also handle the task of carrying out enlightenment campaigns on climate change, cleaner energy and environmental protection within their designated outpost.

POST GRADUATE EMPLOYMENT:

We will as well employ over 500 experienced environmentalists and administrators to work as Country / State coordinators and over 5,000 support staff to work in the capacity of technical assistants, specials advisers and special assistants. Other jobs functions such as personal assistants, secretaries, office clerks, janitors etc. will be created in the course of the project.
