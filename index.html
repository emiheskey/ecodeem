ABOUT ECODEEM

Committed to global sustainable development

We are a social media platform focused on climate action by addressing the cogent issues of climate change, environmental degradation, poverty and unemployment; as we ensure commitments aimed at systematically achieving programs directly linked to the sustainable development goals.

Our Vision

A social media company connecting people around the world to finding pleasure in playing an active role in achieving global sustainable development; even as we remain focused and passionate about (goal 7 and 13 of the SDG's) advocacy and mitigation of climate change, access to affordable and clean energy and the preservation of our global environment.

Our Mission

Using ecodeem to practically engage and connect people around the world to becoming effective solution providers, change agents, environmental protectors and advocates of the sustainable development goals; hence our resolution to execute projects and programs aimed at directly improving the lives of millions of individuals and communities particularly those across Africa, Latin America and Asia which will provide these regions with socio economic benefits, job creation, poverty alleviation, environmental preservation, internally generated revenues for governments, better access to clean energy and a more sustainable pathway prosperity.

Special Features

Trade Now

Helps users with the options of trading their recyclable waste and engage in Carbon trading. Users can easily contact the nearest van by filling in the category and uploading a photo/video of the their waste and the pickup location. This feature can only be accessed when user opens a Green Account.

Green Account

Enables users keep track record of their financial and recycling progress. Your Green Account can also be pass-worded for privacy and security reasons. Points gotten from users waste can converted to money and deposited into each users Green account within 24hrs after collection.

Eco Solution

Provides users a direct access to renewable energy solutions (Solar Panel, Inverter, Power Banks, Batteries, Windmills, Electric Vehicles) that meets their energy needs and preservers our global environment. Users can also buy airtime and as well redeem their monies by transferring into their bank accounts.

Social Media

Provides a social platform for users to interact and share interesting moments together, as individuals can like, comment and share voice notes, pictures and videos. Users can also create groups.

Eco Partners

Provides users the platform to partner with ECODEEM by offering their vans of certain models for the business of recyclable waste collection and in return will be paid certain amount of money on either weekly/monthly basis as agreed.

Green Links

Users can build professional profile and network with professional involved in sustainable development across the world. Users can view and upload sponsored videos for public viewing.

Carbon Footprint Calculator

Helps users make commitment actions towards reducing their levels of carbon emission and getting benefits for such commitments. Users can calculate and keeping daily records of their emission rates.
